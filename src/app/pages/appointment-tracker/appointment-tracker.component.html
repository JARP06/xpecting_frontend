Tips

First Prenatal Visit

Schedule your first prenatal visit as soon as you have a positive home pregnancy test. Good prenatal care is crucial for a healthy pregnancy and baby. The first appointment usually occurs between weeks 6 and 8. Start acting pregnant immediately, such as taking a prenatal vitamin and following a healthy diet. If you suspect a high-risk pregnancy, check with your practitioner for an earlier appointment.

Preparing for Your First Pregnancy Appointment

Before your first prenatal appointment, prepare by:

  • Make a list of key health facts, including personal medical history, mental health history, gynecological history, obstetrical history, and potential disease exposure.
